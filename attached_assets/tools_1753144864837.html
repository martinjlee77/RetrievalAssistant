Our Suite of AI Tools

Revenue Recognition (ASC 606)

Our AI parses sales contracts to identify the 5 steps: identify the contract, separate performance obligations, determine the transaction price (including variable consideration), allocate the price, and define the timing of recognition. Our expert review service validates complex judgments for multi-element arrangements.

Leases (ASC 842)

Input lease terms and let the AI run the five classification tests. It generates the right-of-use asset and lease liability calculations, provides the journal entries, and produces a complete amortization schedule. Our experts can review embedded leases and complex modification scenarios.

Financial Instruments (ASC 320/815/820)

Get guided analysis for classifying debt and equity securities. The AI helps identify potential embedded derivatives that require bifurcation and separate accounting. Our expert service is ideal for complex hedging or fair value level 3 instrument reviews.

Business Combinations (ASC 805)

The AI tool scans deal documents to help you create a preliminary purchase price allocation worksheet, identifying major tangible and intangible assets for valuation. Engage our experts to review the final valuation report and ensure compliance.

Stock-Based Compensation (ASC 718)

Our tool helps you model and calculate compensation expense for equity awards. It handles graded vesting schedules and helps you document your forfeiture rate assumptions. Experts can review complex modifications or performance-based awards.

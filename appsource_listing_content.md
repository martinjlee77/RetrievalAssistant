## Long Description (2000-4000 characters)

**Transform Technical Accounting Analysis from Weeks to Minutes**

VeritasLogic.ai is an enterprise-grade AI platform that helps accounting teams generate professional, audit-ready technical accounting memos across major FASB standards (ASC 606, 842, 718, 805, 340-40). Built for Big 4 firms, large enterprises, and technical accounting departments, our platform combines advanced AI with authoritative guidance to deliver the quality and rigor your stakeholders demand.

**Why Technical Accounting Teams Choose VeritasLogic.ai**

• **Audit-Ready Quality**: Generate memos with proper citations to authoritative FASB guidance, interpretive literature, and contract-specific analysis
• **Multi-Standard Coverage**: Full support for ASC 606 (Revenue Recognition), ASC 842 (Leases), ASC 718 (Stock Compensation), ASC 805 (Business Combinations), and ASC 340-40 (Capitalization)
• **Professional Formatting**: Export polished memos in Word (DOCX) or PDF format, ready for review or client delivery
• **Contract De-identification**: Automated privacy protection replaces party names with generic identifiers before AI processing
• **Fast Turnaround**: Complete analyses that typically take 2-5 days in just 15-30 minutes
• **Built-in Knowledge Base**: Access to complete FASB Codification guidance, AICPA literature, Big 4 thought leadership, and SEC guidance

**How It Works**

1. **Upload Your Contract**: Support for PDF, Word, and multi-document packages
2. **Answer Key Questions**: Guided interview captures essential transaction details
3. **AI Analysis**: GPT-5 powered engine analyzes your contract against authoritative guidance
4. **Professional Memo**: Receive a comprehensive memo with narrative analysis, conclusions, and proper citations
5. **Export & Review**: Download in Word or PDF format for team review and stakeholder distribution

**Enterprise Features**

• Pooled word allowances for team usage
• Multi-user seats with role-based access
• Audit logs and usage tracking
• Priority support with accounting professionals
• Azure OpenAI deployment option (Enterprise plan)
• Custom SLAs for large firms

**Subscription Plans**

• **Professional**: $295/month - 30,000 words, 1 user seat
• **Team**: $595/month - 75,000 words, 3 user seats
• **Enterprise**: $1,195/month - 180,000 words, unlimited seats

All plans include a **14-day free trial with 9,000 words** (credit card required, cancel anytime before day 14).

**Trusted by Technical Accounting Professionals**

VeritasLogic.ai maintains the professional rigor and citation quality that accounting teams require, allowing professionals to focus on judgment, client advisory, and strategic decision-making rather than manual research and drafting.

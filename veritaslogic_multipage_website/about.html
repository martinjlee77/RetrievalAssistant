About Us

VeritasLogic.ai exists to turn predictable, time-consuming technical accounting work into streamlined workflows—so your team can move faster without lowering the bar. We build practical tools that combine AI-powered automation with real-world accounting judgment and clear documentation.

We’re an independent, employee-owned company focused on controllers, CAOs, and technical accounting teams. Our priorities are accuracy, responsiveness, and long-term trust. We ship what teams actually use: standard-specific analysis, audit-ready citations, and support that respects your timelines.

VeritasLogic.ai is led by a former Ernst & Young (EY) audit partner with over 20 years auditing and advising Fortune 500 and high-growth companies on US GAAP, SEC reporting, SOX, and complex accounting matters, with most recent work implementing generative AI in financial reporting and audit.

About Us

VeritasLogic.ai exists to turn predictable, time-consuming technical accounting work into streamlined workflows—so your team can move faster without lowering the bar. We build practical tools that combine AI-powered automation with real-world accounting judgment and clear documentation.

We’re an independent, self-funded company focused on controllers, CAOs, and technical accounting teams. Our priorities are accuracy, responsiveness, and long-term trust. We ship what teams actually use: standard-specific analysis, audit-ready citations, and support that respects your timelines.

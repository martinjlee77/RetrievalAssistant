Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 6, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as your name, email, company details, and uploaded documents for analysis purposes.

2. How We Use Information

Information is used to provide accounting analysis services, manage your account, process billing, and improve our platform.

3. Document Handling

Uploaded documents are processed temporarily and are not stored permanently on our servers. Documents are deleted after analysis completion.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit and at rest.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law or to provide the service.

6. Data Retention

Account information is retained while your account is active. Usage analytics are retained for service improvement purposes.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. Contact us to exercise these rights.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and service functionality. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

9. Changes to Policy

We may update this privacy policy and will notify users of significant changes via email or platform notification.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at privacy@veritaslogic.ai

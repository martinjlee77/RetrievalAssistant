Accounting Standards Currently Supported

Coverage of major accounting standards with built-in knowledge of FASB authoritative guidance.

  • ASC 606: Revenue Recognition
  • ASC 340-40: Contract Costs
  • ASC 842: Leases (Lessee only)
  • ASC 718: Stock Compensation
  • ASC 805: Business Combinations

Roadmap: ASC 815 Derivatives and Hedging in development.

What's excluded: Lessor accounting (ASC 842) and cost to fulfill (ASC 340-40) are not currently supported.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does billing work?

Purchase credits in $500, $1,000, or $2,000 blocks. Credits expire 12 months from purchase and are automatically deducted based on the tier-based per analysis pricing. You're only charged after successful analysis completion. Payments processed by Stripe.

What's included in each analysis?

Every analysis includes a professional technical memo with paragraph-level ASC citations, accounting conclusions, and export to DOCX/PDF/HTML. Includes one complimentary re-run within 14 days for input corrections.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. There are no contracts or commitments. You can stop using the service anytime and your unused credits remain available until the 12-month expiration.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

First-memo refund upon request within 14 days if the draft is not usable for your stated purpose. Manual approval.

How does enterprise pricing work?

Enterprise pricing offers volume discounts, custom integrations, and dedicated support. Contact our sales team for a custom quote based on your expected usage.

How long does analysis take?

3–5 minutes typical for 30-page contract; larger documents may take longer. Optional expert review services are completed within 1-2 business days.

What does the complimentary re-run cover?

Input corrections (e.g., updated contract text, fixed extraction) or minor clarifications. New methodology, new documents, or materially expanded scope typically require a new analysis.

Do you support lessor accounting under ASC 842?

Not currently. We support Lessee only.

Do you store my files?

No. We process your documents in a temporary session and don't store files after analysis. Only an analysis history entry is retained. We use the OpenAI API with data retention turned off—your data isn't used to train models.

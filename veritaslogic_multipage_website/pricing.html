Frequently Asked Questions

How does billing work?

You purchase analysis credits in advance. Credits never expire and can be used for any supported ASC standard. We charge your card automatically when you're running low, or you can manually add credits anytime.

What's included in each analysis?

A complete professional memo with contract analysis, accounting treatment recommendations, journal entries, and supporting documentation. All export formats (DOCX, PDF, HTML) are included.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. There are no contracts or commitments. You can stop using the service anytime and your unused credits remain available for future use.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

Yes. If you're not satisfied with any analysis within 30 days, we'll refund the cost and help you understand how to get better results.

How does enterprise pricing work?

Enterprise pricing offers volume discounts, custom integrations, and dedicated support. Contact our sales team for a custom quote based on your expected usage.

Do you offer academic or non-profit discounts?

Yes. We offer 50% discounts for academic institutions and qualified non-profit organizations. Contact us with your requirements.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does billing work?

Purchase credits in $500, $1,000, or $2,000 blocks. Credits expire 12 months from purchase and are automatically deducted based on document complexity ($3-$30 per analysis). You're only charged after successful analysis completion.

What's included in each analysis?

Every analysis includes a professional technical memo with paragraph-level ASC citations, accounting conclusions, and export to DOCX/PDF/HTML. Includes one complimentary re-run within 14 days for input corrections.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. There are no contracts or commitments. You can stop using the service anytime and your unused credits remain available until the 12-month expiration.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

Yes. For your first analysis only, we offer a full refund if requested within 14 days. We'll refund the analysis cost and help you achieve better results going forward.

How does enterprise pricing work?

Enterprise pricing offers volume discounts, custom integrations, and dedicated support. Contact our sales team for a custom quote based on your expected usage.

How long does analysis take?

Typically 2-5 minutes for analysis and memo completion. Larger documents may take longer. Optional expert review services are completed within 1-2 business days.

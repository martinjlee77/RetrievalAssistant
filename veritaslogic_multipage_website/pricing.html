Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the 14-day free trial?

Your trial includes 20,000 words, which is enough to run full technical accounting analyses on real-world contracts. A payment method is required to start the trial, but you won't be charged if you cancel before the trial ends. After 14 days, your subscription automatically continues at the Professional plan rate ($295/mo) unless canceled.

How does word rollover work?

Unused words from your monthly allowance roll over and remain available for 12 months. For example, if you use 100,000 words in January on the Professional plan, the remaining 50,000 words will carry forward and expire in January of the following year.

What ASC standards are covered?

All plans include full coverage of ASC 606 (Revenue Recognition), ASC 842 (Leases), ASC 718 (Stock Compensation), ASC 805 (Business Combinations), and ASC 340-40 (Contract Costs). ASC 815 (Hedging) is coming soon.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

You can upgrade at any time through your account dashboard. Downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle. Any rollover words you've accumulated will carry forward regardless of plan changes.

Is my contract data secure?

Absolutely. Original uploaded files are not stored. De-identified memo outputs and analysis metadata (file names, dates, sizes, tier, cost) are retained to enable access to your completed analyses and for billing and support purposes. Data shared with OpenAI is not used to train models and it is de-identified before transmission. Your de-identified data is deleted by OpenAI after 30 days for abuse monitoring.

Do you offer annual billing?

Currently we offer monthly billing only. If you require annual billing, contact us at support@veritaslogic.ai.

What if I need more words than my plan includes?

If you consistently need more capacity, you can upgrade to a higher tier at any time. With 12-month rollover, most customers find they build up a buffer that covers occasional large documents. For high-volume needs, contact us about Enterprise plans with custom allowances.

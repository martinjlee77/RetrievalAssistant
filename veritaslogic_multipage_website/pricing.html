Frequently Asked Questions

How does billing work?

You are only charged after an analysis completes successfully. You can pay per analysis or, for convenience, purchase credit blocks. Please note: all credit purchases are final, non-refundable, and non-transferable. To ensure you receive a quality result, every analysis includes a complimentary re-run (see our re-run policy for details).

What's included in each analysis?

Every analysis includes a professional technical memo with executive summary, analyses, paragraph-level ASC citations, accounting conclusions, and export to DOCX/PDF/HTML. It includes one complimentary re-run within 14 days for input modifications or extractable text adjustments. New methodology, new documents, or materially expanded scope typically require a new analysis.

Are there any contracts or commitments?

No. VeritasLogic.ai is a pay-per-analysis service with no long-term contracts or commitments. You can stop using the service at any time. Any unused credits you have purchased will remain in your account until they expire 12 months from their purchase date.

What if I don't have a credit card?

No problem. Email us at support@veritaslogic.ai and we'll explore other payment options.

How does enterprise pricing work?

Enterprise pricing offers volume discounts, custom integrations, and dedicated support. Contact our sales team for a custom quote based on your expected usage.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the 14-day free trial?

Your trial includes 9,000 words (~$295 value), which is enough to run a full technical accounting analysis. A payment method is required to start the trial, but you won't be charged if you cancel before the trial ends. After 14 days, your subscription automatically continues at the Professional plan rate ($295/mo) unless canceled.

How does word rollover work?

Unused words from your monthly allowance roll over and remain available for 12 months. For example, if you use 20,000 words in January on the Professional plan, the remaining 10,000 words will carry forward and expire in January of the following year.

What ASC standards are covered?

All plans include full coverage of ASC 606 (Revenue Recognition), ASC 842 (Leases), ASC 718 (Stock Compensation), ASC 805 (Business Combinations), and ASC 340-40 (Contract Costs). ASC 815 (Hedging) is coming soon.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes! You can upgrade at any time through your account dashboard. Downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle. Any rollover words you've accumulated will carry forward regardless of plan changes.

Is my contract data secure?

Absolutely. We operate on a strict zero-retention policy. Your contract documents are never stored after analysis, and your data is never used to train AI models. All processing is ephemeral, and we maintain enterprise-grade encryption at rest and in transit.

Do you offer annual billing?

Currently we offer monthly billing only. Annual billing with a discount will be available soon. Contact us at support@veritaslogic.ai if you're interested in annual prepayment options.

What if I need more words than my plan includes?

If you consistently need more capacity, you can upgrade to a higher tier at any time. With 12-month rollover, most customers find they build up a buffer that covers occasional large documents. For high-volume needs, contact us about Enterprise plans with custom allowances.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the process work?

Submit your contract or draft memo through our contact form. We'll review it and provide a quote within one business day. Once confirmed, our vLogic AI engine analyzes your documents, and a former Big 4 Partner reviews the output. You receive a professional, audit-ready memo typically within 24-48 hours for standard transactions.

What determines Standard vs. Complex pricing?

Standard memos ($750) cover routine transactions: typical SaaS contracts, real estate leases, straightforward stock option grants. Complex transactions (from $1,500) involve multi-element arrangements, contract modifications, financing transactions, or situations requiring significant professional judgment. We'll assess complexity and provide a quote before starting.

What ASC standards do you cover?

We provide analysis for ASC 606 (Revenue Recognition), ASC 842 (Leases - Lessee), ASC 718 (Stock Compensation), ASC 805 (Business Combinations), and ASC 340-40 (Contract Costs - Cost to Obtain).

What if I need revisions?

One revision round is included with every memo. If you provide additional information or have follow-up questions after delivery, we'll incorporate your feedback at no extra charge.

Is my contract data secure?

Absolutely. We handle confidential contracts daily. Original documents are processed securely and not retained after delivery. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We're happy to sign your NDA or confidentiality agreement before you submit documents.

How do I pay?

We invoice after memo delivery via email. Payment is due within 30 days. For recurring engagements, we can discuss volume arrangements.

Can I get a sample memo first?

Yes. Contact us to request a sample memo or schedule a brief call to discuss your needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the process work?

Submit a request through our contact form. We will send you a secure link to upload your contract or draft memo. We'll review it and confirm pricing within one business day. Once confirmed, our vLogic AI engine analyzes your documents, and a human expert reviews the output. You receive a professional, audit-ready memo typically within 24 hours for standard transactions.

What's the difference between Preparation and Review services?

Both services cost $750 and include AI-powered analysis plus human expert review. Preparation Service: We analyze your contract and prepare a complete technical memo from scratch. Review Service: We analyze your existing draft memo and provide a gap analysis with recommendations. Both include the same level of human expert validation.

What determines if a transaction needs a custom quote?

The $750 price covers standard transactions: typical SaaS contracts, routine leases, straightforward stock option grants, and standard business combinations. Complex transactions requiring custom quotes include: complex arrangements, contract modifications, M&A transactions, financing transactions, or unusually long contracts. While most contracts should fall into the standard pricing, we'll assess complexity and provide a custom quote before starting.

What ASC standards do you cover?

We provide analysis for ASC 606 (Revenue Recognition), ASC 842 (Leases - Lessee), ASC 718 (Stock Compensation), ASC 805 (Business Combinations), and ASC 340-40 (Contract Costs - Cost to Obtain).

What if I need revisions?

One revision round is included with every memo. If you provide additional information or have follow-up questions after delivery, we'll incorporate your feedback at no extra charge.

Is my contract data secure?

We handle confidential contracts daily. Original documents are processed securely and not retained after delivery. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We're happy to sign your NDA or confidentiality agreement before you submit documents.

How do I pay?

For standard memos ($750), we send a secure Stripe payment link along with your final draft invoice. Payment is due upon receipt. For custom engagements or enterprise clients, we offer Net 30 terms.

Can I get a sample memo first?

Yes. Contact us to request a sample memo or schedule a brief call to discuss your needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does billing work?

Run an analysis and pay the per-analysis price for the tier that matches your document size. You’re only charged after the analysis completes successfully. Payments are processed by Stripe. For convenience, you can purchase $1,000, $2,000, or $3,000 credit blocks. Credits are deducted automatically at the same tier rates and expire 12 months from purchase

What's included in each analysis?

Every analysis includes a professional technical memo with executive summary, analyses, paragraph-level ASC citations, accounting conclusions, and export to DOCX/PDF/HTML. It includes one complimentary re-run within 14 days for input corrections.

What does the complimentary re-run cover?

Input corrections (e.g., updated contract text, fixed extraction) or minor clarifications. New methodology, new documents, or materially expanded scope typically require a new analysis.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. There are no contracts or commitments. You can stop using the service anytime and your unused credits remain available until the 12-month expiration.

What if I don't have a credit card?

No problem. Email us at support@veritaslogic.ai and we'll explore other payment options.

How does enterprise pricing work?

Enterprise pricing offers volume discounts, custom integrations, and dedicated support. Contact our sales team for a custom quote based on your expected usage.
